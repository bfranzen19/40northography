tools

i use adobe lightroom and, sometimes photoshop, purely to recreate the colors and sharpness of real life in the photographs and, occasionally, to give the images a similar look to watercolor paintings. i never photoshop objects into/out of my images.



working with wild women

i volunteer a lot of my time working with women's outdoors organizations. i have had the opportunity to take pictures for the US women's whitewater rafting team in their preparation to represent the US at the world championships in japan. i have also photographed bell's joyride - a program in which women get to mountain bike with and learn from women professional mountainbikers.
